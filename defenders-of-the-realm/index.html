📜 Defenders of the Realm - Rules

🎯 Win Condition

Victory: Defeat all 4 generals (Balazarg, Gorgutt, Varkolak, Sapphire)

💀 Lose Conditions (ANY triggers defeat)

  • General Reaches Monarch City: If any general moves to Monarch City
  • 5 Minions on Monarch City: If 5 minions TOTAL (any combination) occupy Monarch City
  • 12 Taint Crystals: If all 12 taint crystals are placed on the board
  • 25 Minions of Any Color: If all 25 tokens of any single faction are deployed and more are needed

Lose conditions can be individually toggled in ⚙️ Settings.

⚔️ War Status

The war escalates as generals fall. Each phase increases the number of Darkness Spreads cards drawn per Night Phase:

  • Early War (start): Draw 1 Darkness card per Night Phase
  • Mid War (1st general defeated): Draw 2 cards — Card 2 is general advance only (minion placements skipped)
  • Late War (3rd general defeated): Draw 3 cards — Card 3 is general advance only

🔄 Turn Structure (3 Phases)

Each hero's turn ends with three sequential phases:

  • Step 1 — Daytime: Hero takes damage from minions at their current location
  • Step 2 — Evening: Draw 2 hero cards. If over 10 cards, must discard down to 10
  • Step 3 — Night: Draw Darkness Spreads card(s) based on War Status. Cards are shown one at a time with a preview, then resolved individually

🌙 Darkness Spreads (Night Phase)

Each Darkness card is previewed before resolution. The preview shows warnings for:

  • Overrun: If a 4th minion of the same color would be placed, it triggers an overrun — minions spread to connected locations
  • Taint Crystal: If a location overflows, a taint crystal is placed
  • General Advance: Whether the general will move toward Monarch City or be blocked
  • General-Only Cards: In Mid/Late War, extra cards skip minion placements and only advance generals

💎 Overrun & Taint Mechanics

  • Overrun (4th Minion Rule): When a 4th minion of the same color would be placed at a location, a taint crystal is placed and the excess minions spread to each connected location
  • Taint Crystals: Placed when locations overflow with minions. 12 total taint crystals triggers a lose condition
  • 🌳 Heal the Land: Remove taint crystals by spending matching cards at the location

🎮 Actions

Actions per turn = Life tokens. Each hero has a different amount of life tokens (e.g. Paladin has 6, Eagle Rider has 4). Taking wounds reduces both life and available actions.

  • Heroes start with 2 cards
  • Each turn: draw 2 cards during Evening phase
  • Hand Limit: Maximum 10 cards — must discard excess after drawing
  • Death at 0 life tokens: Discard all cards, respawn at Monarch City

Action Tray Buttons:

  • 🥾 Foot: Walk to an adjacent connected location (1 action per space)
  • 🐎 Horse: Discard a matching horse card to travel 2 spaces
  • 🦅 Eagle: Discard a matching eagle card to fly 4 spaces
  • 🌀 Magic Gate: Travel between magic gate locations (free if at a gate, or discard a Magic Gate card from anywhere)
  • 💫 Build Magic Gate: Spend an action + Magic Gate cards to build a permanent gate at your location
  • 🍺 Rumors: At an Inn, draw 2 cards (costs 1 action, usable twice per turn)
  • 🌳 Heal the Land: Spend matching cards to remove a taint crystal at your location
  • ❤️ Healing Wounds: Heal 1 wound per action at a safe location (no minions/generals) OR full heal at an Inn or Monarch City for 1 action
  • ⚔️ Engage Minions: Roll dice to fight minions at your location (1 die per matching card)
  • 👹 Attack General: Fight a general at your location (only when no minions present). Discard matching cards to roll dice
  • 📜 Quests: View your quest cards and attempt to complete quests at the required location
  • 🌟 Special Cards: Play a purple Special Card from any hero's hand for its unique effect
  • ⏭️ End Turn: End your turn and begin the 3-phase turn resolution

Some heroes have additional action buttons (🔥 Fireball, 🏹 Archery, ✝️ Sanctify Land, 🗡️ Crafty) that appear when conditions are met. See hero ability details in the Heroes modal.

📜 Quest Cards

  • Each hero draws 1 quest card at game start
  • Quests require traveling to a specific location and spending 1 action to attempt a dice roll
  • The 🎯 Complete Quest button appears on the action tray when at a quest location
  • On success: the quest reward activates and a new quest is drawn from the deck
  • On failure: most quest cards are discarded and a new one is drawn. Some quests (like War Banner of Valor) can be reattempted
  • Quest types:
    • Magic Items (passive): Amulet of the Gods (+1 combat dice), Boots of Speed (+2 actions), Helm of Power (+1 card draw), War Banner of Valor (ignore defeat penalties)
    • Use-Anytime (active): Crystal of Light (remove taint at your location), Ancient Tree of Magic (remove taint from any location). These stay on the hero after completion and are discarded when used
    • Placeholder quests: Can be completed but reward not yet implemented
  • Use the 📜 Quests button to view quest details, see the location on the map, or use completed quest cards

⚔️ Combat

  • Minions & Generals: Hit requirements vary by faction:
    • 🟢 Orcs (Green): 3+ to hit (easier)
    • ⚫ Undead (Black): 4+ to hit
    • 🔴 Demons (Red): 4+ to hit
    • 🔵 Dragons (Blue): 5+ to hit (harder)
  • Generals: Each general has unique health. Use matching faction cards to roll dice.
  • Group Attacks: Multiple heroes at the same location can coordinate attacks on a general
  • Failed Minion Attacks: Take 1 wound when failing to kill all minions
  • Failed General Attacks: Each general has unique defeat penalties (wounds + card loss). The War Banner of Valor quest item negates these penalties

👹 General Combat Skills

  • Balazarg (Red/Demons): Demonic Curse — Roll D6 per card before battle, discard card on 1. Defeat penalty: D3 wounds + ALL cards
  • Gorgutt (Green/Orcs): Parry — Each 1 rolled eliminates 1 hit. Defeat penalty: 2 wounds + 2 cards
  • Varkolak (Black/Undead): No Re-rolls — Heroes cannot re-roll dice. Defeat penalty: D6 wounds + D6 cards
  • Sapphire (Blue/Dragons): Regeneration — Returns to full health if not defeated in one attack. Defeat penalty: 3 wounds + D6 cards

⚔️ General Wounds & Healing

  • Minor Wounds: When a general takes damage but survives. Healing begins after a 1 turn delay.
  • Major Wounds: A more severe injury. Healing delayed until a full round of player turns completes. Generals with Major Wounds cannot advance!
  • Healing Rate: Once healing begins, generals heal +1 HP at the end of each player's turn.
  • Major Wound Thresholds: Gorgutt at ≤2 HP, Balazarg at ≤1 HP. Varkolak cannot have major wounds.
  • Sapphire: Ignores the wound/healing system entirely due to Regeneration.

🌟 Special Cards

  • Purple cards in the hero deck are Special Cards with unique effects.
  • Special cards can be played at any time without using an action via the 🌟 Special Cards button.
  • Alternatively, they can be used in general combat for their dice value against their specified faction.
  • Any hero can play a special card held by any other hero.
  • 💫 Magic Gate: Place a Magic Gate on any location (2 dice vs Undead in combat).
  • 🔨 Hammer of Valor: Move any hero to any location (2 dice vs any General in combat).
  • ✨ Spell of Purity: Remove all Tainted Crystals from a single location (2 dice vs Demons in combat).
  • 🏹 Elven Archers: Remove all enemy minions from 2 Green locations (1 die vs Orcs in combat).
  • ⚔️ Battle Strategy: Defeat all minions on any 3 locations, then push 1 General back a location (2 dice vs any General in combat).
  • 🌅 All Is Quiet: Skip Darkness Spreads — no cards are drawn this turn (2 dice vs Orcs in combat).
  • 🍀 Battle Luck: During any combat, re-roll all failed dice. Cannot be used against Varkolak (1 die vs any General in combat).
  • 💥 Battle Fury: Defeat all enemy minions at the active hero's current location. Requires 1 Action. (2 dice vs Dragonkin in combat).
  • 📜 Local Information: At an Inn, draw 5 cards — keep all that match a chosen color and all Special cards (1 die vs any General in combat).
  • 👑 King's Guard Attack: Remove up to 6 minions on or adjacent to Monarch City (1 die vs Dragonkin in combat).
  • 🐎 Cavalry Sweep: Sweep through adjacent locations removing up to 6 minions (max 2 per location). Dragonkin count as 2. (1 die vs any General in combat).
  • 🔮 Dark Visions: View top 5 Darkness Spreads cards. Discard any to avoid, return rest in chosen order (1 die vs any General in combat).
  • 🛡️ Militia Secures Area: During Night Phase, cancel 1 minion placement from a Darkness Spreads card (1 die vs Undead in combat).
  • 🏰 Strong Defenses: During Night Phase, prevent the General from moving on a Darkness Spreads card (1 die vs Demons in combat).
  • 👤 Spy In The Camp: During Daytime, prevent 1 wounded General from healing for the active hero's turn (1 die vs any General in combat).

🗺️ Map Features

  • Colored Paths: Show general movement routes to Monarch City
  • Purple Locations: Monarch City and Inns (special locations)
  • 🌀 Magic Gates: Teleport between gate locations or build new ones
  • 💎 Taint Crystals: Corrupted locations shown on map tooltips
